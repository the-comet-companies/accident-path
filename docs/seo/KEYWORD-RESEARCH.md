## 3. PEOPLE ALSO ASK (PAA) QUESTIONS

These questions appear in Google's "People Also Ask" boxes for PI queries. Each one represents a featured snippet opportunity. PAA snippets can drive 8-15% CTR even for new domains.

### Car Accident PAA Clusters

**Trigger query: "car accident"**
- What do I do immediately after a car accident?
- Should I call the police for a minor accident?
- How long do I have to report a car accident?
- Can I sue if the accident was partly my fault?
- What happens if the other driver doesn't have insurance?
- How much can I get for a car accident settlement?
- Do I need a lawyer for a car accident?
- What should I tell my insurance company after an accident?

**Trigger query: "car accident lawyer"**
- How much does a car accident lawyer cost?
- Do I need a lawyer for a minor car accident?
- What percentage does a personal injury lawyer take?
- How do I find a good car accident lawyer?
- When should I hire a car accident lawyer?
- Is it worth getting a lawyer for a car accident?
- Can I switch lawyers during my car accident case?
- What questions should I ask a car accident lawyer?

**Trigger query: "car accident settlement"**
- How much is the average car accident settlement?
- How long does it take to settle a car accident claim?
- What factors determine a car accident settlement amount?
- Should I accept the first settlement offer?
- How are pain and suffering calculated?
- What is the average settlement for a rear-end collision?
- Do most car accident cases settle out of court?
- What is the average settlement for whiplash?

### Truck Accident PAA

**Trigger query: "truck accident"**
- Who is liable in a truck accident?
- How are truck accidents different from car accidents?
- What federal regulations apply to truck drivers?
- Can I sue the trucking company?
- What is the average truck accident settlement?
- How long do truck accident cases take?
- What evidence is important in a truck accident case?
- What should I do if hit by a commercial truck?

### Motorcycle Accident PAA

**Trigger query: "motorcycle accident"**
- Is the motorcycle always at fault?
- What are the most common motorcycle accident injuries?
- Does wearing a helmet affect my claim?
- How much is a motorcycle accident settlement?
- Can I still get compensation if I wasn't wearing a helmet?
- What to do at the scene of a motorcycle accident?

### Slip and Fall PAA

**Trigger query: "slip and fall"**
- How do I prove a slip and fall case?
- What is the average slip and fall settlement?
- How long do I have to file a slip and fall claim?
- Can I sue if I slipped on a wet floor?

- Who is responsible for a slip and fall accident?

### Insurance Claims PAA

**Trigger query: "insurance claim after accident"**
- How long do I have to file an insurance claim after an accident?
- What happens if the other driver's insurance denies my claim?
- Should I give a recorded statement to the insurance company?

- What if the insurance settlement is too low?
- How does uninsured motorist coverage work?

### Injury-Specific PAA

**Trigger query: "whiplash injury"**
- How long does whiplash last?
- What is the average whiplash settlement?

- How do you prove whiplash from an accident?

- What is the average TBI settlement?

- What are the long-term effects of TBI from a car accident?

## 9. SERP FEATURE ANALYSIS

### Keywords with Google AI Overviews (SGE)

Google AI Overviews (formerly SGE) appear for many informational PI queries, providing synthesized answers. This reduces click-through rates on organic results by an estimated 15-30%. Strategy: Be cited as a source in the AI Overview by having authoritative, well-structured content.

| Keyword Category | AI Overview Presence | Impact on CTR | Strategy |
|-----------------|---------------------|---------------|----------|
| "what to do after [accident type]" | Almost always present | -20-30% CTR reduction | Structure content as numbered steps; aim to be cited source |
| "how much is [case type] worth" | Usually present | -25-35% CTR reduction | Provide ranges by state/severity; include data AI will cite |
| "[accident type] lawyer" | Rarely (commercial intent) | Minimal impact | Focus on content quality signals |
| "who is at fault [scenario]" | Often present | -15-25% CTR reduction | Provide definitive answers with state-specific nuance |
| "statute of limitations [state]" | Almost always present | -30-40% CTR reduction | State-specific table format; be the cited source |
| Comparison keywords ("X vs Y") | Often present | -20-30% CTR reduction | Provide clear, structured comparison tables |
| Calculator/tool keywords | Rarely (tool intent) | Minimal impact | Tools cannot be replaced by AI text; major advantage |
| Spanish PI keywords | Rarely present | Minimal impact | Spanish AI Overviews are much less developed |

**Key insight:** Tool-based and interactive content is the most AI-Overview-resistant content type. Google cannot synthesize an interactive calculator or checklist in an AI Overview. This further validates the tool-first strategy.

## APPENDIX A: KEYWORD DIFFICULTY BENCHMARKS

Understanding what "competition" means for a new domain (DA 0-10):

| Competition Level | What It Means | Can a New Domain Rank? | Timeline |
|------------------|--------------|------------------------|----------|
| Very Low | Few quality pages target this keyword; thin/outdated results | Yes, within 2-4 weeks | Fast |
| Low | Some quality pages but not comprehensive coverage | Yes, within 1-3 months | Moderate |
| Low-Medium | Mix of authoritative and thin results | Yes with great content, 2-4 months | Moderate |
| Medium | Several strong pages from authoritative domains | Possible with exceptional content + time, 4-8 months | Slow |
| Medium-High | Strong competition from high-DA domains | Difficult; requires links + time, 6-12 months | Very slow |
| High | Dominated by DA 70+ domains (Nolo, FindLaw, Avvo) | Very difficult; requires significant authority, 12-18+ months | Long game |
| Extreme | Dominated by DA 90+ domains + heavy ad spend | Not realistic for a new domain in the first year | Do not target initially |
