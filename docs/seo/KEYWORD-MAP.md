## Notes

- **KD (Keyword Difficulty):** 0-30 = easy for new domain, 30-50 = medium, 50-70 = hard, 70+ = very hard
- **Priority should favor KD < 30 keywords in months 1-3** for a new domain
- Spanish keywords typically have 5-10x less competition than English equivalents
- Tool pages can rank on utility even without high DA — prioritize these early
- City pages with Local Pack presence need Google Business Profile to compete
