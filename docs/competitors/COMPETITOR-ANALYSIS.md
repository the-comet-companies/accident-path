## WHAT EVERY COMPETITOR DOES WRONG (OUR OPPORTUNITY)

### 1. ZERO Educational Content
Not a single competitor has substantive educational content. No guides, no "what to do after an accident," no injury explainers. They are all lead-capture forms with thin marketing copy. **This is the #1 opportunity.** Google rewards depth. Users reward value.

### 2. NO Interactive Tools
Zero competitors have calculators, checklist generators, case quizzes, injury journals, or any interactive utility. They are all forms. **Our 10 tools are a massive differentiator.**

### 3. Terrible Design (All of Them)
Every site looks like it was built in 2015 or earlier. Wix templates, generic WordPress themes, cluttered navs. Not a single site looks like a premium SaaS product. **A Stripe/One Medical level design immediately signals credibility.**

### 4. Vague Referral Process
None explain HOW matching works. "Submit your info and we'll call you" is the universal pattern. No transparency on attorney vetting, matching criteria, or timeline. **Full transparency = massive trust advantage.**

### 5. No Attorney Profiles
Not a single competitor shows who their panel attorneys are, their credentials, specialties, or track records. Users are asked to blindly trust. **Showing attorney profiles (even anonymized) builds confidence.**

### 6. Weak Trust Architecture
Most rely on a single "State Bar Certified" badge. No client reviews, no case outcomes, no attorney review dates on content. **Layer trust signals on every page.**

### 7. Poor Mobile Experience
Multiple competitors have navigation issues on mobile. Dropdowns with 200+ items, overlapping sticky elements, small touch targets. **Mobile-first design is table stakes in 2026.**

### 8. No Content Freshness
No competitor has a blog, news section, or dated content. Content appears static and potentially years old. **Fresh, dated content signals to Google that the site is actively maintained.**

### 9. Minimal Disclaimers
Most have boilerplate disclaimers buried in footers. None have per-page contextual disclaimers or clear disclosure about how attorney matching works. **Proactive compliance = trust + legal protection.**

### 10. No Post-Intake Value
After form submission, the user experience ends. No confirmation page with resources, no "while you wait" content, no follow-up tools. **Post-intake value keeps users engaged and builds loyalty.**
