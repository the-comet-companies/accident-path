# Legal Funding Integration Strategy

## THE OPPORTUNITY

Pre-settlement legal funding is a **$2-4 billion industry** growing 15-25% annually. It's a natural add-on to a PI accident platform because the same people who need a lawyer also need money while they wait for their case to settle.

**Why this fits perfectly:**
- Same customer (injured person with a pending PI case)
- Same moment (right after the accident, before settlement)
- Same trust relationship (they came to us for help)
- Adds massive value to the consumer (financial relief during hardship)
- Creates additional revenue stream beyond attorney referrals
- Deepens attorney partnerships (attorneys love when clients aren't desperate to settle)

## HOW LEGAL FUNDING WORKS (For Content)

### The Consumer Journey

```
Accident → Injury → Hire Attorney → Case Filed → WAITING (months to years)
                                                      ↑
                                            FINANCIAL PRESSURE:
                                            - Can't work
                                            - Medical bills piling up
                                            - Rent/mortgage due
                                            - Insurance lowballing

                                            → PRE-SETTLEMENT FUNDING
                                              solves this problem
```

### Typical Funding Amounts

| Case Type | Typical Advance | % of Expected Settlement |
|-----------|----------------|------------------------|
| Auto accident (soft tissue) | $500 - $5,000 | 10-15% |
| Auto accident (surgery/fracture) | $2,000 - $25,000 | 10-15% |
| Slip and fall | $1,000 - $10,000 | 10-15% |
| Wrongful death | $10,000 - $100,000+ | 10-15% |

### Why Consumers Need It
- PI cases take 1-4 years to settle
- Injured people often can't work during recovery
- Medical bills, rent, food — life doesn't stop during a lawsuit
- Without funding, plaintiffs settle too early for too little
- Insurance companies exploit this desperation

## INTEGRATION MODELS (SAFEST TO MOST INTEGRATED)

### Model A: Educational Content Only (SAFEST — DO THIS FIRST)

**How it works:** Add a "Legal Funding" section to the site with educational content explaining what pre-settlement funding is, how it works, pros/cons, what to watch out for.

**Pages to build:**
- `/resources/legal-funding` — What is pre-settlement funding?
- `/resources/legal-funding/how-it-works` — Step-by-step process
- `/resources/legal-funding/pros-and-cons` — Honest breakdown
- `/resources/legal-funding/questions-to-ask` — Before you sign
- `/resources/legal-funding/alternatives` — Other options to consider

**Revenue:** None directly from this model. Value is in:
- More content = more SEO traffic
- Trust building = higher conversion on attorney matching
- Establishes authority in the PI space

**Compliance risk:** VERY LOW — pure education

---

### Model B: Advertising/Sponsorship (LOW RISK)

**How it works:** Rock Point (or other funders) pay for advertising placement on the site. Display ads, sponsored content sections, or "Featured Partner" placements.

**Implementation:**
- "Legal Funding Partners" section on relevant pages
- Rock Point banner/card on intake results page
- Sponsored content clearly labeled as advertising
- Rock Point pays a flat monthly ad fee (NOT per-referral)

**Revenue:** $2,000-$10,000/month depending on traffic volume

**Compliance risk:** LOW — standard advertising relationship
- MUST clearly label as advertising/sponsored
- MUST NOT imply editorial endorsement
- Ad fee must be flat (not per-click or per-referral to avoid kickback concerns)

---

### Model C: Warm Handoff / Co-Marketing (MODERATE RISK)

**How it works:** After a consumer completes intake and is matched with an attorney, offer them information about legal funding as an optional next step. If interested, warm-transfer to Rock Point.

**Implementation:**
- After intake results: "While your case is pending, you may qualify for financial assistance."
- Educational explainer + optional "Learn More" that connects to Rock Point
- Consumer explicitly opts in (not auto-referred)
- Track conversions for reporting but don't charge per-referral

**Revenue:** Negotiate a marketing services agreement with Rock Point
- Monthly retainer for marketing services
- NOT per-referral fee (compliance risk)

**Compliance risk:** MODERATE
- Must NOT accept per-referral payments from funder
- Must have clear consumer disclosure
- Must have attorney acknowledgment in the flow
- NEEDS legal counsel review before launching

---

### Model D: Embedded Marketplace (HIGHER RISK — FUTURE)

**How it works:** Build a legal funding marketplace into the platform where consumers can compare multiple funders, apply directly, and track their funding status.

**Implementation:**
- Multiple funder partners (Rock Point + others)
- Comparison tools (rates, terms, reviews)
- Direct application through our platform
- Case status tracking
- Attorney dashboard integration

**Revenue:** Technology/SaaS fees from funders for platform access
- $500-2,000/month per funder for marketplace listing
- Premium placement fees
- API access fees for application processing

**Compliance risk:** HIGHER
- May require CFL (California Finance Lenders Law) license from DFPI
- Need legal opinion on whether platform is "brokering" loans
- Full disclosure requirements
- Consumer protection compliance
- REQUIRES legal counsel before building

## COMPLIANCE RULES FOR LEGAL FUNDING CONTENT

### SAFE Language
```
"Pre-settlement funding is a financial option some plaintiffs use while their case is pending."
"If you don't recover money in your case, you typically owe nothing back."
"Legal funding companies charge fees that can be significant. Understand all costs before signing."
"Discuss legal funding options with your attorney before applying."
"This information is educational. We are not a legal funding company."
```

### PROHIBITED Language
```
"You should get legal funding" (financial advice)
"Legal funding is risk-free" (misleading — there ARE costs)
"Apply now for instant cash" (predatory marketing)
"Guaranteed approval" (no funder can guarantee)
"Best rates" (unverifiable claim)
```

### Disclosures Required
1. We are NOT a legal funding company
2. We do not provide financial advice
3. Legal funding has costs that may be significant
4. Consumers should consult their attorney before applying
5. If showing a partner funder: clearly labeled as advertising/sponsorship
6. Funding terms vary by provider and case
