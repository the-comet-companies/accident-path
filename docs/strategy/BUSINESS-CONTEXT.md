## 2. Revenue Model

### Best Near-Term Model

Charge partner law firms for:

| Revenue Stream | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| Marketing-qualified leads | Per-lead pricing for vetted intake leads |
| Signed cases | Fee per signed case under approved state structures |
| Geographic exclusivity | Monthly fee for exclusive territory |
| Intake as a service | Outsourced intake for law firms |
| Call center / CRM / records | Operational services for partners |
| Content syndication | White-labeled tools and content |

**The cleanest early model:** Marketing + intake services, not "we take a cut of every case in every state." Whether percentage-based compensation is allowed varies by structure and jurisdiction.

### More Ambitious Model (Future)

Form or partner into an **Arizona ABS** (Alternative Business Structure) and operate with:
- Compliance officer
- Supervising Arizona lawyer
- Intake QA
- State-by-state routing rules
- Formal fee-sharing and disclosure workflows where permitted

Arizona's ABS framework exists for this innovation, but it is regulated, audited, and not a substitute for multi-state legal review.
